Design Model


Our IT company embraces a holistic approach to workspace design, ensuring every aspect contributes to productivity and employee satisfaction. From the welcoming reception area where guests are greeted with warmth and efficiency, to the comfortable restrooms and guard room ensuring security, every space is carefully curated. Our manager room fosters collaboration and decision-making, while the project organizer room promotes efficient workflow management.

The CEO room exudes leadership and vision, inspiring the team towards shared goals. Our kitchen serves as a hub for relaxation and socialization, fostering camaraderie among colleagues. The work area is designed for optimal functionality, equipped with state-of-the-art technology to support innovation and creativity. Finally, the meeting room provides a dedicated space for brainstorming sessions and client interactions, facilitating effective communication and idea exchange. Together, these elements form a cohesive environment where our team can thrive and excel in delivering cutting-edge IT solutions.

CypherSpark

Project


Our project aims to revolutionize Sri Lanka's public transportation system by implementing a comprehensive GPS tracking system for both SLTB and private buses, accessible through a user-friendly mobile application.

This innovative solution provides passengers with real-time information on the exact location of buses, ensuring convenience and reliability in their travel plans. By integrating live tracking technology, commuters can effortlessly monitor the current whereabouts of buses, minimizing wait times and enhancing overall transit experiences. Whether navigating through urban centers or rural landscapes, our GPS system ensures that passengers stay informed and connected, offering a seamless journey from departure to arrival. With this initiative, we aspire to streamline transportation logistics, improve commuter satisfaction, and contribute to the modernization of public transit infrastructure across Sri Lanka.
